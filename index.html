Yu-Gi-Oh! SuperDB

AI-Powered Yu-Gi-Oh! Card Database

About

Yu-Gi-Oh! SuperDB is a powerful tool that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to answer virtually any question related to Yu-Gi-Oh! cards, card sets, and banlists in both the Trading Card Game (TCG) and Official Card Game (OCG).

Simply ask questions in natural language, and get precise answers drawn from a comprehensive Yu-Gi-Oh! database - no SQL knowledge required!

How to Use

  1. Copy the super prompt by clicking here
  2. Head to ChatGPT, paste the prompt and type your question
  3. Click the link ChatGPT gives you to get the answer

Example Questions

  • What are the sets that contain normal monsters?
  • List the cards that have been banned in the TCG sorted by the duration for which they were banned.
  • What are the couples of monster mirrors? A monster mirror is a monster that has DEF equal to the ATK of the first monster; ATK equal to the DEF of the first monster; and level equal to 12-x where x is the level of the first monster and opposite attribute (LIGHT↔DARK; WIND↔EARTH; WATER↔FIRE).
